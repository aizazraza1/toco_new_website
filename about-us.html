About us

We believe protection should be straightforward, transparent, and built around your needs.

Whether it’s your daily commute, your family’s home, or your weekend adventures, Toco keeps you covered from life’s costly surprises.

Get to know Toco

At Toco, our mission is simple: protect what you value most without complexity or large upfront costs.

We pioneered the industry’s first month-to-month subscription model, making high-quality coverage accessible to more people, without the burden of long-term contracts. Whether it’s keeping your car on the road, your home running smoothly, or preparing for your next adventure on the water — we make sure you have a clear plan and a reliable team ready to step in.

Our goal is simple: keep you protected, wherever life takes you.

Coverage that fits your life

Our plans protect you from costly repairs and essential service interruptions that can disrupt your day-to-day:

  • Auto

    From the engine to electrical systems, with benefits like roadside assistance, rental reimbursement, and trip interruption coverage.

  • Home

    Protection for key systems and appliances, helping you avoid the stress and expense of emergency repairs.

  • Toco at Work

    Employer-sponsored coverage that gives employees valuable protection benefits, including vehicle, home, and future marine coverage options.

  • Marine (coming soon)

    Coverage designed to keep your time on the water smooth and worry-free.

Our Commitment, Recognized
