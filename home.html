Get Covered Now
1

Start Your Claim

Quick & Easy Filing

Start your claim effortlessly through our online portal or connect with our team for personalized assistance. Our user-friendly platform and dedicated support staff ensure a smooth beginning, guiding you through each step with clarity and efficiency.

Regardless of how you choose to file, we are prepared to handle your claim promptly, working toward a quick resolution.

2

Repair or Replace?

Flexible Repair and Replacement Options

Once your claim is authorized, you can move forward with a repair or replacement based on our assessment.

Here’s what to expect:

Select Your Technician

You can choose your own technician - giving you control over who services your home; or we can connect you with certified professionals from our trusted network.

Seamless Replacement Process

If a replacement is the best option, we’ll walk you through securing a new item. You have the flexibility to obtain it in the way that suits you best, whether online, in-store, or through another preferred method.

Comprehensive Support

No matter which option you choose, our team is here to assist with documentation, authorization, and logistics, ensuring the process remains as smooth as possible.

3

Submit Your Receipt

Effortless Receipt Submission

Submitting your receipt is quick and easy with our automated system. Simply upload your documentation through our intuitive platform, and our advanced review system will handle the rest.

Designed for convenience, this process ensures your claim is reviewed accurately and efficiently.

4

Get Reimbursed!

Fast and Reliable Reimbursement

Enjoy the benefit of swift reimbursement through our efficient, automated system. Once your claim is approved, we prioritize rapid processing, ensuring funds are delivered directly to you with minimal delay.

Our focus is on closing your claim quickly and efficiently, so you can move forward with confidence.
