If you own a car, you know how costly unexpected repairs can be. With Toco’s month-to-month plan, major repairs are covered so you can get back on the road without draining your bank account.

  • No down payment

How it works (and why it’s so easy)

Three simple steps. And a team that’s with you all the way.

Car trouble? Call us.

Check engine light? Strange noise? We’re here for you. Our team will confirm what’s covered and help you start a claim — no guesswork, no stress.

We’ll handle the repair

Choose any licensed mechanic, contractor, or marine tech you trust. Need a recommendation? We’ve got your back. We’ll coordinate directly with them to approve the work — fast and hassle-free.

Toco pays the bill

Once your repair is authorized, you cover your deductible—we cover the rest, directly. No reimbursements, no delays. Just peace of mind.

Here's why Toco makes sense

The rising costs of car repairs can be overwhelming. We are here to help manage these expenses effectively.

  • Extended Ownership Costs

    More drivers are keeping their cars longer, which means higher long-term repair and maintenance expenses.

  • Average Car Maintenance Costs

    Drivers spend $500–$700 a year on upkeep. For older cars, costs can exceed $1,000 annually.

  • Car repair costs are up almost 20%

    Repair prices rose over six times faster than inflation last year—one of the biggest spikes in household costs.
